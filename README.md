Management Reports Dashboard
Project Overview
Management Reports Dashboard is a frontend-based web application developed to visualize and present 10 key management reports for business monitoring and decision-making.

The application focuses on clean UI design, structured reporting, and consistent color theming, providing a clear and user-friendly dashboard experience for management users.

Tech Stack
HTML5
CSS3
JavaScript
Report Components (10 Modules)
Daily Revenue & Ride Summary
Displays overall revenue and ride count trends.

Driver Performance Report
Visual representation of driver activity and efficiency.

City-wise Business Report
Breakdown of business metrics by city.

Customer Growth Report
Tracks customer acquisition and engagement.

Cancellation & Service Quality Report
Highlights cancellations and service issues.

Payment Summary Report
Displays payment method distribution.

Driver Incentive Report
Shows incentive eligibility and summaries.

Operational Efficiency Report
Monitors operational performance metrics.

Marketing Performance Report
Tracks offers and campaign performance.

Profitability Overview Report
High-level view of costs versus revenue.
